## Expected Results for Sample 1:

When you paste Sample 1, the webhook should extract:

{
  "rl1": {
    "A": 60000,
    "F": 425,
    "B.A": 3200.50,
    "H": 8500
  },
  "t4": null,
  "rrsp": 5000,
  "slipType": "RL-1",
  "success": true
}

Then TaxSyncQC should auto-fill:
- Box A: 60000
- Box F: 425
- RRSP: 5000

And when you click "Estimate Credits", you should see approximately:
- Total Benefit: $5,802
- Cash Refund: $728
